FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about the organization, what we are building, and what comes next.

What is Rockstar Health?
A nonprofit-owned mobility company making adaptive accessories now and working toward larger rehabilitation technology. More on the About page.
Is Rockstar Health currently selling wheelchairs?
Not yet. We are early, starting with adaptive accessories while the larger work develops. See our products.
What makes Rockstar Health different?
User control, fast repairs, transparency, and nonprofit ownership. The mission spells it out.
Is Rockstar Health a nonprofit?
Yes. Rockstar Health Organization, Inc. is the nonprofit parent, and it owns the manufacturing subsidiary, Rockstar Health Industries Corporation. Full breakdown on financial structure.
Where is Rockstar Health based?
Saint Johns, Florida.
Can I support the organization?
Donation pathways, partnerships, and volunteer opportunities are coming as we grow. Reach out and we will keep you posted.
