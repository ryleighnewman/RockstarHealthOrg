FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about the organization, what we are building, and what comes next.

What is Rockstar Health?
Rockstar Health is a nonprofit-owned mobility equipment organization focused on developing adaptive equipment and future rehabilitation mobility technology.
Is Rockstar Health currently selling wheelchairs?
Not yet. The organization is currently in early-stage development and initial accessory rollout phases.
What makes Rockstar Health different?
Rockstar Health is being built around user control, modular repairability, transparency, and nonprofit ownership.
Is Rockstar Health a nonprofit?
Rockstar Health Organization, Inc. is the nonprofit parent organization. Rockstar Health Industries Corporation operates as the manufacturing subsidiary.
Where is Rockstar Health based?
Saint Johns, Florida.
Can I support the organization?
Future donation pathways, partnerships, and volunteer opportunities will be announced as the organization expands.
